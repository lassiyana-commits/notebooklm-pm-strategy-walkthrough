# Prompts for Step 1
Create a new notebook. Click on Add Sources. Paste the below prompt entirely and select the Menu combinations Web and Fast Search/Deep Search.

```
Search recent transcripts, filings, or product keynote summaries from these three foundational anchors:
Microsoft: Focus on enterprise Copilot adoption and software pipelines.
Google: Focus on Gemini integrations across consumer & Workspace.
Apple: Focus on Apple Intelligence, local silicon, & privacy moats.

```
